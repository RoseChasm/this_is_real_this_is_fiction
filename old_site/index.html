Let me introduce myself,

My real name is Michaela Stone Cross.

My fake name is Rosalie C. Chasm.

I write code like I write poetry: badly.

I write code like I write poetry: with relish.

Call me an artist. Call me a writer. Call me a coder, a designer, a journalist. Call me all but not one.

Above all I am a storymaker. Some of the stories are even good. And some of them are even real.

I am making this site not just to put up my real and fake stories-- I am making this site to help reinvent what it means to be a writer, to reinvent what it means to be a builder of imaginary worlds.

And what is the internet but the greatest imaginary world there is?
